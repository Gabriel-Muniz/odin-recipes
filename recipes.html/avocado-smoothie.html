Avocado Smoothie

Description

Savor the simplicity of our avocado smoothie, crafted with ripe avocados and enriched with powdered milk. This creamy concoction offers a smooth, velvety texture and a rich, satisfying taste. Perfect for a quick and nutritious treat, it's a delightful way to enjoy the pure, natural goodness of avocados.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place milk, avocado, yogurt, and honey into a blender; add ice cubes and blend until smooth.
